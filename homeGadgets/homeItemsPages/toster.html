Sunbeam Wide Slot 4-Slice Toaster, Black
Product description:

Make toast just the way you like with the Sunbeam Wide Slot 4-Slice Toaster. This sleek black
toaster features Electronic Toasting Technology that delivers consistent, even results and
extra-wide slots that allow you to toast bagels, sliced bread, and all other types and sizes of breads.
This bread toaster is equipped with turn knobs that offer seven toast shade options
from light to dark as well as pre-programmed Bagel settings. Separate toast controls allow simultaneous toasting at toaster off in case of a jam for
safe, hassle-free operation, while a convenient cancel button allows you to stop the toasting process at any time. A toast-lift lever allows you to retrieve your food easily, and a removable crumb tray makes cleaning quick and easy. Built to North
American Electrical Standards, the Sunbeam Wide Slot 4-Slice Toaster is backed by a one-year limited warranty.
